    • Gaming/Tech

    Since I was a young kid I was always interested in technology and gaming was a huge part of it. I didn't just enjoy getting new tech, I wanted to know how it worked so everytime I got something new I would immediately begin to take them apart. Growing up in a small town there really wasn't much to do so I really got into gaming during my teen years. I would play sports in the summer and become a gamer during the winter. I built my first computer at 15 then everything just grew from there. I enjoy keeping up with all the new hardware, gaming, streaming, and generally learning about any new product.
